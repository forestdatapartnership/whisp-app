### Regular Data Processing

Whisp allows users to submit geo-data, including vector geometries, which may be processed via Google Earth Engine (GEE), a third-party cloud service managed by Google. Data transmitted to GEE may be temporarily cached and processed in cloud-based data centers. FAO does not store or retain user-submitted geo-data.

### Sensitive Data Processing

The geometry submitted by users shared with GEE is not identifiable to any particular individual. The processing of users' geo-data for Whisp is instead carried out on servers maintained in Europe. FAO will not store or retain users' geo-data.

FAO protects user privacy when accessing geo-data by transmitting only approximated geographic information to GEE, along with additional masking data to prevent identification of the actual area of interest. This information is used solely to facilitate processing and is not permanently stored or retained by GEE.

Tabular data linked to users' geographical information is only temporarily cached and is not permanently stored. This data is not shared with GEE at any stage of the processing workflow.
